Assistant Product Specialist

  1. Home
  2. Careers
  3. Jobs
  4. Assistant Product Specialist

We are primarily a Software Development agency specializing in building high-scale cloud-native SaaS and enterprise applications. Our customers range from startups to large publicly-listed enterprises across the US and Europe.

We are looking for a self-starting, entrepreneul professional to join our team and help and support the Product Specialist or work independently in discovering and improving a product’s market fit. You will work with Product Specialists, stakeholders, and clients to define and document product’s vision, features, and user experience.


Objectives of this Role
  • Work with clients and other stakeholders to clearly define and prioritize product roadmap keeping in mind long-term business goals
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with engineering and design to steer product roadmap
  • Translate business requirements to technical requirements and innovative solutions
  • Align teams to a shared product vision and steer execution of that vision
  • Take over collecting end-to-end feedback from customers, development, and other relevant stakeholders
  • Build strong product insights through consistent customer engagement and market research and contribute to product vision
  • Be in the details. Know your team's work and partner team’s work in-depth and understand the impact for your team and stakeholders
  • Take logical, data-driven decisions on product features that are highly influenceable on customer engagement and conversion
Responsibilities
  • Understand product features and help create a product backlog based on user feedback and current market trends
  • Drive and ensure the prioritization and description of the product backlog as part of the team
  • Define features through detailed specifications, user stories, and wireframes
  • Be accountable for coordinating and fostering involved teams
Skills and Qualifications
  • Independent and curious, you set your targets, ask for help when you need it and are always looking to learn something new
  • Strong drive to help the customers succeed
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Great attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Enthusiastic, proactive, likes to take initiative & work in a challenging environment
  • Knowledge of marketing and product positioning a plus
  • Knowledge of software engineering processes a plus
  • A degree in business or software engineering is a plus
Pay Range:
3.6-4.8 LPA CTC
Relevant Experience

0-3 years of relevant experience
