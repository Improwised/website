Improwised Technologies is a leading IT company founded with a desire to develop innovative software solutions in 2011. Since then, the company has relentlessly worked toward its goal of empowering its clients with cutting-edge solutions and proficient services.

Two technopreneurs Rakshit Menpara and Shailesh Davara are the building blocks of this company and run it with the best industry standards and highest business values. The company has a team of passionate developers, designers, and other IT engineers that help their clients realize the true potential of the products. The company is renowned for its cloud applications and SaaS solutions. Furthermore, this top software company is a technical partner of many global companies catering to their technology support-related needs.

Why us?

Build Future Ready Systems

We believe in developing solutions that can give you a competitive edge now and then. We use cutting-edge technologies and the latest tools in the process of developing state-of-the-art solutions to keep you, your system, and people future-ready.

Client-Centric Approach

For us, our clients and their best interest are the only priority. We don’t look at our revenues when it is about benefiting our clients and never hesitate to recommend an off-shelf solution over our full-time employment services when needed.

Complete Transparency

We believe in keeping our clients informed during all stages of development and support. We use advanced communication tools such as Slack to provide hassle-free and quick communication with your hired technical engineers.

Early Adoption of Automation

Automation in various stages and processes has its benefits. We usually adopt automation from the inception of any project to deliver positive benefits of monitoring, ongoing delivery, etc. to our clients to provide the best ROI and experience.

Highly Versatile Team

Our team believes in taking ownership of the project and each member of the team is well versed in their work of design, development, and support. Our team keeps itself upgraded to benefit our clients with the latest industry innovations.

One Stop Solution

We believe in providing you with end-to-end services by removing the hassle of the multi-vendor model. Our bottom-to-top IT services will serve as your organizational backbone, allowing you to focus all of your energies on growth.
